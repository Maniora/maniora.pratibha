Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the path to clarity.

I'm a student/professional—is this really for me?

Yes. Whether you are 15 choosing a stream or 25 choosing a career pivot, the core challenge is the same: Clarity. I help you align your natural strengths with your next big move, regardless of your life stage.

How is this different from talking to a mentor or friend?

A mentor gives advice based on their experience; a friend tells you what they think you want to hear. I use professional methodologies to help you discover what YOU actually want and how to get there.

Do I have to do a lot of homework? I'm already busy.

The goal is to reduce your load, not add to it. Most of the "work" happens during our conversations. Any outside actions are small, strategic, and designed to give you instant relief.

What if I'm not ready for a major life change yet?

You don't need a huge "Life Reset" to benefit. Often, the best results come from small, tactical pivots in how you think and work. We move at your pace.

What results should I honestly expect after 3 months?

A clear roadmap, a quiet mind, and a deep sense of confidence in your daily choices. Most clients report that the "mental fog" disappears, allowing them to excel in their chosen path.

What really happens during the free Clarity Call?

It's a zero-pressure, 20-minute conversation. We chat about where you're stuck, and I give you my honest professional opinion on the best next step for you. No sales pitch—just clarity.
